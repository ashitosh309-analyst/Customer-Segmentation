# Customer-Segmentation

•	Summary :
          The goal is to analyse over [2834] rows of transaction data to categorize customers into distinct distinct behavioural segments. This allows the marketing team to stop “blasting” the entire database and start sending targeted offers to the right people.

•	Objectives :

1.	Checking unique values.
2.	Which city has the highest number of sales in USA?
3.	Which coutnry has the highest number of sales?
4.	Which is the best product in United States of America (USA)?
5.	Gruop of sales by productline.
6.	What was the best month for sales in a specific year? How much was earned that month?
7.	November seems to be the best month, what product do they sell in November?
8.	Who is our best customer (this could be best answered with RFM)?
9.	Customer Segmentation.
